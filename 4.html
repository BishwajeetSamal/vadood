Blue Rabbit enables the effortless administration of healthcare through an ecosystem of technology driven solutions and services. We’re committed to giving veterinary teams new superpowers to create positive health outcomes.

Our vision is to empower veterinarians to deliver better health and financial outcomes by creating a highly integrated and customized approach to disease management that combines innovative technology, workflow management, and proactive patient engagement into one powerful ecosystem.

Partnering with vets to help patients–and practices–thrive.

Blue Rabbit is a leading provider of healthcare technology and services for companion and equine veterinary practitioners. Leveraging proprietary workflows and technology to ensure complete, personalized treatment protocols, we are revolutionizing the coordination of care for patients and the veterinarians who serve them.

PASSIONATE ABOUT PATIENT OUTCOMES

Blue Rabbit is fiercely determined to remove obstacles, help vets build healthy businesses and deliver better outcomes for the beloved pets and horses they care for.

DRIVEN BY INNOVATION

Our innovative technologies simplify the coordination of disease management, improving treatment protocol compliance and enabling holistic patient care. We’re committed to providing new insights, new tools, and new capabilities that enable veterinarians to dramatically improve the quality of care and patient outcomes, particularly for those with urgent and complex medical needs.

PARTNERS IN TRUST

We view our relationships as true partnerships. We will be there every step of the way to support our veterinary partners, help them grow their practices, serve their clients and ultimately heal their patients. Our relationships are built on trust and respect, and we work diligently to earn both.

Who We Are.

Our executive leadership team brings to market decades of experience in the animal health industry. We strive to be thought leaders in healthcare technology, pharmacy operations, prescription management, customer service and client engagement. In an industry where siloed solutions can result in complexity and confusion for anxious pet and horse owners, our team is dedicated to bringing easy-to-administer personalized medicine and care to veterinarians and their clients.
